1983

In 1983, we had an idea. We wanted to do research on humans and see how they responded to colors. Late Russian scientist Alexei I. Ivanov helped out with our experimentation. Ivanov is part of the reason why our company exists today.



1999

Our tests had generally positive results, so we started planning to make a true location and make our technology readily available to customers. David Miller, CEO of the company at the time, was cruetial in the planning stage and helped develop blueprints for our first location



2013

In 2013, we built our very first location in Stamford, Connecticut. We are thankful to have discovered this technology and make it readily available to those who need it.
